DAWN ROMANCE



Near black everywhere
Light will come at the right time
Called Romance of Dawn



Explanation

At night people are in their own houses staying still, resting,
and minding their own business but when a ray of light comes
after hours of darkness with a little moonlight,
People rhythmically always do the same thing and that is waking up
others go to work and others doing their own things.
It is called "Romance" but not connected romantically however connected in some other ways.
Dawn gives us chance to start over again, light with a little bit of shadow that's what actual romance is.
The beginning of a new day and thus a chance for happiness and improvement "Will of Dawn" that's what it's called.
